5. Feature Engineering Strategy

Engineered Clinical Features

High Risk Profile

High_Risk_Profile = (BMI > 35) & (Age > 45) & (HighBP == 1)

Identifies metabolic syndrome indicators

Multiple Conditions Score

Multiple_Conditions = HighBP + HighChol + HeartDisease + Stroke >= 2

Quantifies comorbidity burden

Age-BMI Risk Assessment

Age_BMI_Risk = (Age × BMI) / 100

Compound risk factor for diabetes progression

9. Approach 2 - Medical Domain-Informed Strategy

Case Filtering Methodology

Clear Diabetes Cases Criteria

clear_diabetes = ( ((BMI > 35) & (Age > 45)) | ((HighBP == 1) & (HighChol == 1) & (BMI > 30)) | (HeartDiseaseorAttack == 1) )

Clear Healthy Cases Criteria

clear_healthy = ( ((BMI < 25) & (Age < 35)) | ((HighBP == 0) & (HighChol == 0) & (GenHlth <= 2)) )
